Imperium - teaser

blender, premiere pro & after effects, 3 days - 2022

Imperium is a game centered around a society under the watchful eye and control of a dictatorial government employing AI. The eye, or "vision" symbolism permeates many elements of this game, and we aimed to underscore it prominently in the teaser through the dolly-zoom effect, albeit it may be somewhat overused. Additionally, other narrative and gameplay pillars, such as control over information, are depicted through monochromatic colors, except for red, drawing attention to data transfer and violence.
