Death of internet - teaser

Unreal Engine 5, premiere pro & after effects, 3 days - 2023

In collaboration with a sound designer we created this teaser for our master's second year studies project.

As the game is a walking simulator focused on contemplation and voyeurism, shots were thoughts to showcase the environment, and an ever growing tension to tease the narrative aspect.

Glitch, internet and electronics are strong intention pillars, and have been used thoughout the whole teaser to create coherence.

Noutic'Zik - volunteers recruitment

premiere pro & after effects, 4 days - 2023

promotion video aimed to recruit volunteers for a music festival, but also to announce and hype the regular festival goers about the 2023 edition.

While the 2022 edition was canceled, a strong energy struck the festival-goers and volunteers. I tried to capture this energy to transform it into hope and motivation for the year 2023.

Imperium - teaser

blender, premiere pro & after effects, 3 days - 2021

Imperium is a game centered around a society under the watchful eye and control of a dictatorial government employing AI.

The eye, or "vision" symbolism permeates many elements of this game, and we aimed to underscore it prominently in the teaser through the dolly-zoom effect, albeit it may be somewhat overused.

Additionally, other narrative and gameplay pillars, such as control over information, are depicted through monochromatic colors, except for red, drawing attention to data transfer and violence.
